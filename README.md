# SehatMate — MVP  
### Doctor-First Chat Consults & Clinic Workflow

**SehatMate** is a bilingual (**English & Urdu**) doctor-first chat marketplace and clinic workflow tool for **Pakistan**.

Patients select a doctor, pay a small fee, and receive a **text consult or scheduling** directly from that doctor.  
Clinics keep control.

> ⚠️ **Important Disclaimer**  
> This system provides **preliminary advice and scheduling support only**.  
> It does **NOT** provide medical diagnosis and does **NOT** replace licensed medical professionals.

---

## What It Will Do

### Doctor-First Chat Consults
- Patients message doctors after paying a small consult fee  
- Doctors decide whether to:
  - Reply via text  
  - Schedule a clinic visit  
  - Request a call  
- Clinics reduce waiting-room crowding  
- Doctors gain **time-leveraged income**

---

## Core Product Vision  
### (Doctor-First Pivot)

---

## Stage 1 — MVP

- Doctor directory with:
  - Consultation fee  
  - Short professional profile  

- Patient flow:
  - Select doctor → Pay (manual payflow) → Chat session unlocked  

- Doctor tools:
  - Inbox / dashboard  
  - Accept, reply, schedule, or close sessions  

- Backend:
  - CSV logging for events  
  - Payments ledger for manual payouts  

- Compliance:
  - Explicit consent before every session  
  - Disclaimer displayed on **every screen**

---

## Stage 2 — Near-Term

- Easypaisa / JazzCash paylinks (merchant onboarding)
- Scheduling & follow-up automation
- Simple analytics:
  - Sessions
  - Revenue
  - Conversion rates

---

## Stage 3 — Long-Term

- Escrow-based payments & automated payouts
- Clinic integrations:
  - White-label installs
  - EMR hooks
- Advanced features (AI assists)
  - **Only after sufficient labeled data**
  - **Only after legal & regulatory review**

---

## Philosophy

- Doctor-first, not platform-first  
- Clinics retain authority  
- Payments are transparent  
- Scale **only after trust**
